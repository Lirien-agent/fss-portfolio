## What We Do

### 1. Prospect Identification

Not generic lists. We research exactly who should be talking to your company right now.

- Industry mapping by niche, company size, geography, and tech stack
- Decision maker identification: CEO, CTO, VP of Sales, Head of Operations, Procurement Manager
- Buying signal filters: recent funding, headcount growth, new product launches, job postings that indicate pain
- Access to 275M+ verified B2B contacts across industries and regions

**Output:** A curated list of prospects with name, title, company, buying signal identified.

---

### 2. Contact Enrichment

A name without contact data is useless.

- Corporate email verification (bounce rate <5%)
- Full LinkedIn profile, company tech stack, team size
- Personalization notes: something specific about that person or company that justifies the outreach
- Waterfall enrichment: if one data provider doesn't have it, we automatically try the next

**Output:** A contact ready to message — no manual research needed.

---

### 3. Personalized Multichannel Outreach

No templates. Real messages that sound like they were written by someone who actually did their homework.

- **Cold email** — subject lines that get opened, bodies that get responses, out of the spam folder
- **LinkedIn DM** — shorter, more direct, better for founders and C-level
- **Cold calls** — used as a sequence step after email/LinkedIn warm-up
- **SMS/WhatsApp** — where permitted, especially effective in non-US markets

AI pulls context from the prospect's LinkedIn, company website, and recent news — and automatically weaves it into the message. Real personalization at scale.

**Output:** A first message that doesn't look like spam — because it isn't.

---

### 4. Automated Follow-Up Sequences

80% of deals close between the 5th and 8th contact. Most people give up after the 2nd.

- 4-8 step sequences per prospect (email + LinkedIn + call + variations)
- Smart spacing: persistent without being annoying
- Different angle on every touch — not the same message three times
- Automatic domain warm-up to protect email deliverability
- Exit condition: positive reply, negative reply, or no response after N attempts

**Output:** A system that works 24/7 without manual follow-ups.

---

### 5. Meeting Preparation & Close Support

When a prospect responds, you need to be ready.

- One-pager tailored to the specific prospect
- Pitch deck personalized by industry and use case
- Proposal with clear pricing options
- Pre-meeting brief: who they are, what they care about, likely objections

**Output:** You walk into the meeting with an advantage — not improvising.

---

### 6. Positioning & Messaging Strategy

Sometimes the problem isn't the outreach — it's that the company isn't positioned to win large contracts.

- External perception audit: how does your company look to someone who's never heard of you?
- Gap identification in messaging, value prop, or use case clarity
- Specific recommendations: what to change on the website, in the pitch, in the copy
- Repositioning support to compete for enterprise deals

**Output:** A company ready to compete for bigger contracts.

## Industries Where This Works Best (USA & Global)

### SaaS & Technology
The most competitive market — but also the one with the most structured buying. Decision makers are reachable on LinkedIn, budgets are defined, and there's a clear procurement process.

**Decision maker:** CTO, VP of Engineering, Head of Product, Director of IT  
**Main pain:** Can't build fast enough internally; need trusted vendors  
**Best channel:** LinkedIn + cold email

### Professional Services (Legal, Accounting, Consulting)
High-ticket deals, low outreach competition. Most firms rely 100% on referrals and have no systematic acquisition channel — which means the playing field is open.

**Decision maker:** Partner, Managing Director, Practice Lead  
**Main pain:** No pipeline outside of word-of-mouth  
**Best channel:** LinkedIn + formal email

### E-commerce & Retail
Brands and retailers looking to scale digital presence, automate operations, or enter new markets.

**Decision maker:** CEO, CMO, Head of E-commerce, Brand Manager  
**Main pain:** Revenue plateaued, channels saturated, need new growth vectors  
**Best channel:** Email + LinkedIn

### Logistics & Supply Chain
Global pressure on logistics hasn't let up. Automation and optimization are urgent priorities.

**Decision maker:** VP of Operations, Director of Logistics, COO  
**Main pain:** Manual processes at scale, high error rate, rising costs  
**Best channel:** Direct email + cold call

### Healthcare & MedTech
Long cycles but large contracts. Regulatory-aware outreach with clear ROI framing wins.

**Decision maker:** VP of Clinical Operations, Director of IT, CMO  
**Main pain:** Efficiency and compliance gaps  
**Best channel:** Email + warm introduction

### Education & HR Tech
Post-pandemic demand for digital learning and workforce tools hasn't slowed.

**Decision maker:** VP of HR, Director of L&D, Chief People Officer  
**Main pain:** Scale without proportional budget increase  
**Best channel:** LinkedIn + email
